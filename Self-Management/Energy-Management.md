# Energy Management

## Overview

Managing your energy, not just your time, for peak performance throughout the day.

## Energy Cycles

### Ultradian Rhythms
- 90-120 minute cycles
- Peak performance periods
- Natural rest periods
- Work with your body's rhythm

### Circadian Rhythms
- Daily energy patterns
- Morning person vs. night owl
- Identify your peak hours
- Schedule important work accordingly

## Energy Mapping

### Identify Your Patterns
- Track energy levels throughout the day
- Note when you're most focused
- Identify low-energy periods
- Plan activities accordingly

### Energy Types
- **Physical**: Body energy
- **Emotional**: Mood and feelings
- **Mental**: Focus and clarity
- **Spiritual**: Purpose and meaning

## Time-of-Day Optimization

### Morning
- High cognitive tasks
- Important decisions
- Creative work
- Deep work

### Afternoon
- Meetings
- Administrative tasks
- Routine work
- Collaboration

### Evening
- Planning
- Reflection
- Learning
- Light tasks

## Recovery Strategies

- Regular breaks
- Power naps
- Movement breaks
- Breathing exercises
- Nutrition timing

## Peak Performance

- Identify your peak hours
- Protect peak time
- Schedule important work during peaks
- Use low-energy time for routine tasks
