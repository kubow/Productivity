# Digital Minimalism & Distraction Management

## Overview

Reducing digital distractions and creating a healthier relationship with technology.

## Digital Minimalism Principles

### Intentional Technology Use
- Use technology with purpose
- Avoid mindless consumption
- Set clear boundaries
- Regular audits

### Digital Decluttering
- Remove unused apps
- Organize digital files
- Clean up subscriptions
- Reduce digital noise

## Distraction Management

### Notification Management
- Turn off non-essential notifications
- Batch notifications
- Use Do Not Disturb
- Schedule notification times
- Priority notifications only

### App Management
- Remove distracting apps
- Use app timers
- Block distracting sites
- Organize by purpose
- Regular app audits

### Focus Modes
- Do Not Disturb
- Focus mode settings
- Airplane mode for deep work
- Website blockers
- Phone-free zones

## Social Media Management

### Boundaries
- Time limits
- Specific times for checking
- Remove from phone
- Use web version only
- Unfollow/unsubscribe

### Consumption Habits
- Intentional scrolling
- Quality over quantity
- Curate feed
- Limit time
- Regular breaks

## Email Management

- Check at specific times
- Batch processing
- Unsubscribe regularly
- Use filters
- Inbox zero

## Phone Management

### Healthy Phone Habits
- No phone in bedroom
- Phone-free meals
- Put away during work
- Use grayscale mode
- Delete distracting apps

### Digital Detox
- Regular breaks
- Tech-free days
- Vacation from devices
- Reconnect with offline activities

## Information Diet

- Curate sources
- Quality over quantity
- Set consumption limits
- Regular audits
- Unsubscribe from noise
- For reading strategies and source evaluation, see [Reading & Information Consumption](../Personal-Development/Reading-Information-Consumption.md).
