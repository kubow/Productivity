# Financial Productivity

## Overview

Managing finances effectively to reduce stress and support productivity goals.

## Budgeting Systems

### 50/30/20 Rule
- 50%: Needs
- 30%: Wants
- 20%: Savings and debt

### Zero-Based Budgeting
- Every penny assigned
- Income minus expenses equals zero
- Detailed planning

### Envelope System
- Cash-based budgeting
- Categories in envelopes
- Digital envelope systems

## Expense Tracking

- Track all expenses
- Categorize spending
- Identify patterns
- Find savings opportunities
- Use apps or spreadsheets

## Financial Planning

### Emergency Fund
- 3-6 months expenses
- Build gradually
- Keep accessible

### Debt Management
- Debt payoff strategies
- Snowball vs. avalanche
- Prioritize high-interest debt

### Savings Goals
- Short-term goals
- Long-term goals
- Retirement planning
- Investment basics

## Financial Goals

- Set financial goals
- Track progress
- Regular reviews
- Adjust as needed

## Money-Saving Strategies

- Reduce recurring expenses
- Negotiate bills
- Buy quality over quantity
- Avoid lifestyle inflation
- Automate savings
