# Habit Systems

## Overview

Building and maintaining productive habits using evidence-based systems.

## Habit Formation

### The Habit Loop (Charles Duhigg)
- **Cue**: Trigger for the habit
- **Routine**: The behavior itself
- **Reward**: Benefit from the habit

### Atomic Habits (James Clear)
- **1% better every day**
- Focus on systems, not goals
- Identity-based habits
- Small changes compound

## Habit Stacking

- Link new habits to existing ones
- "After [current habit], I will [new habit]"
- Build chains of habits
- Start with tiny habits

## Breaking Bad Habits

- Identify the cue
- Understand the reward
- Replace the routine
- Make it difficult
- Remove triggers

## Habit Tracking

- Track daily habits
- Use habit trackers
- Don't break the chain
- Review regularly
- Celebrate streaks

## Identity-Based Habits

- Focus on who you want to become
- "I am a person who..."
- Small wins build identity
- Consistency over intensity

## Common Productive Habits

- Morning routine
- Exercise
- Reading
- Meditation
- Journaling
- Planning
- Review
