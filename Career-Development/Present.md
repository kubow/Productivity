## Preparation
- Start with WHO (Who is the audience)
- Possible Storylines
    - Create interest
    - Spotlight an issue
    - Change stance or mindset
    - Inspire to take action
    - Inform
    - Celebrate
- Key Point (Manage emotional filter of your audience)
- Then continue WHY
    - What are we presenting
    - What it needs to be told (The Key takeaway!)
    - How do we tell it
- Key thought (What is the appropriate flight level of details for your audience?)
- Key point (When you are thinking of a story, give it an emotional shape. It'll help in creating a plot that really works)


## The act of Presenting

- Opening (Smile, Be quiet for a moment)
    - Ask a question (Have you ever asked a question....)
    - Imagine ...
    - Tell a micro-story (Once upon a time... 5-8 sentences)
    - Show a picture
    - State fact
    - Current affairs
- Body
- Closing
    - Point is at the end
    - Anchor the WHY and summarize the key ideas
    - Offer a leaving gift
