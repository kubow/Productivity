# Email & Communication Management

## Overview

Managing email and communication effectively to reduce overwhelm and improve response times.

## Email Management

### Inbox Zero
- Process to zero regularly
- Archive, delete, or act
- Use folders/labels strategically
- Unsubscribe from unnecessary emails

### Email Processing Workflow
1. **Delete**: Unnecessary emails
2. **Delegate**: Forward to appropriate person
3. **Respond**: Quick replies (< 2 minutes)
4. **Defer**: Schedule for later
5. **Do**: Take action if needed

### Email Templates
- Common responses
- Meeting requests
- Follow-ups
- Introductions

### Email Rules and Filters
- Automate sorting
- Priority inbox
- Unsubscribe management
- Spam handling

## Communication Protocols

- Response time expectations
- Communication channels (when to use what)
- Meeting vs. email vs. chat
- Status updates

## Meeting Management

### Effective Meetings
- Clear agenda
- Defined outcomes
- Time limits
- Action items

### Meeting Templates
- Agenda format
- Action item tracking
- Follow-up protocols

### When to Decline
- No clear purpose
- No agenda
- Can be handled via email
- Not relevant to you
