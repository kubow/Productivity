# Reading & Information Consumption

## Overview

Effective strategies for reading, processing, and managing information in the digital age.

## Reading Strategies

### Active Reading
- Engage with the text
- Ask questions
- Take notes
- Summarize key points
- Connect to existing knowledge

### Speed Reading
- Techniques and methods
- Skimming and scanning
- Chunking

### Reading Methods
- Preview before reading
- Read with purpose
- Take breaks
- Review after reading
- Apply what you learn

- Credibility assessment
- Bias detection
- Fact-checking

- Curate sources
- Avoid information overload

- Choose what to read
- Skip irrelevant content
- Focus on value
- Time limits
- Regular pruning
