# Stress Management Techniques

## Overview

Practical techniques for managing stress to maintain productivity and well-being.

## Breathing Techniques

### Box Breathing
- Inhale 4 counts
- Hold 4 counts
- Exhale 4 counts
- Hold 4 counts
- Repeat

### 4-7-8 Breathing
- Inhale 4 counts
- Hold 7 counts
- Exhale 8 counts
- Repeat

### Deep Breathing
- Belly breathing
- Slow and controlled
- Focus on breath
- Calm the nervous system

## Physical Techniques

### Progressive Muscle Relaxation
- Tense and release muscles
- Work through body
- Reduce physical tension

### Stretching
- Desk stretches
- Regular movement
- Release tension
- Improve circulation

### Exercise
- Regular physical activity
- Stress relief
- Endorphin release
- Better sleep

## Mental Techniques

### Mindfulness
- Present moment awareness
- Non-judgmental observation
- Reduce rumination
- Increase clarity
- See also [Meditate](./Meditate.md) for meditation practice and resources.

### Cognitive Reframing
- Challenge negative thoughts
- Find alternative perspectives
- Reframe situations
- Reduce stress response

### Time Management for Stress
- Prioritize effectively
- Set boundaries
- Say no when needed — see [Saying No](../Interpersonal-Development/Saying-No.md) for clear, professional ways to decline
- Avoid overcommitment

## Lifestyle Factors

### Sleep
- Adequate sleep
- Regular schedule
- Quality rest

### Nutrition
- Balanced diet
- Avoid excessive caffeine
- Stay hydrated
- Regular meals

### Social Support
- Talk to others
- Seek help when needed
- Build relationships
- Don't isolate

## Work-Life Balance

- Set boundaries
- Take breaks
- Disconnect regularly
- Protect personal time
- Vacation and rest

For deeper coverage of boundaries, recovery, and integration strategies, see [Work-Life Integration](./Work-Life-Integration.md).
